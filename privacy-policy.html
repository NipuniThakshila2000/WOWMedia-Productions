Privacy policy

WOW Media Productions - Privacy Policy.

Last updated: 27th January 2023.

Website policy

WOW Media Productions (Pvt) Ltd operates wowmedia.lk and uses personal information only for providing and improving the site. Information may include contact-identifying details such as name, plus standard log data such as IP address, browser, visited pages, date, time and site statistics.

Information collection and use

While using the site, WOW Media may ask for personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify users. The site may use third-party services such as Google Analytics to collect, monitor and analyze data.

Communications and cookies

Personal information may be used for newsletters, marketing or promotional materials. Cookies may be used to collect information, and users can instruct their browser to refuse cookies.

Security

The policy states that the site is protected with a Sectigo SSL certificate, while noting that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Mobile app policy

WOW Media Productions is listed as the WMP app developer. The app may collect basic account information including name, email, phone number and profile photo. Account deletion can be requested from the app profile.

Changes and contact

The policy may be updated periodically. Continued use after changes are posted constitutes acknowledgement and consent to the modified policy. Questions can be directed to WOW Media Productions.
